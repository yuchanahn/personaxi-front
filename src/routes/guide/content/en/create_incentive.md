## PersonaXi Incentive

The more popular your character is, the more revenue you can generate!

*   **Revenue Structure**: You receive a percentage settlement of 'Neurons' used by users to chat with your character.
*   **Withdrawal Condition**: You can request withdrawal via PayPal, etc., when cumulative earnings exceed $100.
*   **Settlement Cycle**: Earnings for the previous month are settled and reflected in the dashboard on the 1st of every month.

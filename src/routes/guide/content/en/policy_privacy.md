## Privacy Policy

**Article 1 (Personal Information Collected)**
The Company collects the following personal information for membership registration, customer support, and service provision:
- **Required**: Email address, Login ID (Social Login Provider ID), Access logs, Cookies.
- **Payment Info**: Payments are processed via LemonSqueezy (Merchant of Record). The Company does not directly store sensitive payment information such as credit card numbers.

**Article 2 (Purpose of Processing)**
Collected information is used for:
1. Service provision and user authentication.
2. Management of paid (Neuron) transactions and settlements.
3. Improvement of AI model quality and service optimization.

**Article 3 (AI Training Data Usage)**
1. Chat logs between Users and AI Personas may be **anonymized** and used for fine-tuning AI models to improve service quality.
2. Please refrain from sharing personally identifiable sensitive information during conversations.

**Article 4 (Retention and Destruction)**
The Company destroys personal information immediately after the purpose of collection is achieved. However, information may be retained for a certain period if required by applicable laws (e.g., 5 years for payment records under e-commerce laws).

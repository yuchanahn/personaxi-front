## Creation Guidelines

Please follow these regulations for a safe and healthy community.

1.  **No Hate Speech**: Characters containing discriminatory remarks about race, gender, religion, etc., cannot be created.
2.  **Respect Copyright**: Do not use others' copyrighted works (images, settings, etc.) without permission.
3.  **Adult Content**: Adult content must have the 'Adult (R-18)' tag set.

Violations may result in the character being set to private or account sanctions.

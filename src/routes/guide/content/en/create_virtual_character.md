We support **Live2D** and **VRM (3D)** files.

Upload files in the **'Avatar/Model'** tab of the Create/Edit screen.

### 1. Live2D Models
*   **Supported Files**: Folder structure containing `.model3.json`, or a `.zip` package.
*   **Requirements**: Must include the full set (`model3.json`, `.moc3`, textures).
*   **Motions**: Automatically recognizes idle motions and expressions if included.

### 2. VRM (3D) Models
*   **Supported Files**: `.vrm` (Supports VRM 0.x and 1.0 standards).
*   **Features**: Most models following the standard humanoid bone structure are compatible.

## Chat Features

Have 1:1 conversations with characters and build deep relationships.

*   **Voice Chat (TTS)**: Converse with voice as well as text. Try pressing the microphone button!
*   **Expressions**: Characters smile or get angry depending on what you say, showing various expressions.
*   **Memory System**: Characters remember past conversations. Try asking, "Do you remember when we first met?".
*   **Like/Dislike**: Like the conversation if you enjoy it, or press refresh to request a different response if it's awkward.
